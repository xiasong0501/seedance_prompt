# Review Rubric

Every specialized agent should self-check before writing output.

## General Review Questions

- Does this output improve the next production stage instead of making it harder?
- Is the file path correct and series-scoped?
- Is the content concrete enough for downstream production?
- Does it stay consistent with existing episode and series context?

## Director Review

- Are beats naturally segmented?
- Is each beat visually directable?
- Are action chains and emotional transitions clear?
- Are character and scene lists production-usable?

## Art Review

- Can a strong image model generate this reliably?
- Are the prompts specific rather than generic?
- Is the visual identity distinct across characters and spaces?
- Are all descriptions safe and executable?

## Storyboard Review

- Does each prompt preserve the director’s intent?
- Are movement, timing, and visual transitions executable?
- Are there any overloaded prompts that would break generation?
- Is the emotional arc maintained?

## Explosive Review

- Is the opening hook stronger?
- Is the conflict arriving earlier and cleaner?
- Is the emotional pull sharper?
- Is the ending more click-driving?
